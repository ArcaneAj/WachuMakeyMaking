# Can't decide what to make with what you'v got?

"/wymm" will open a window with 3 tabs:


* Resources
  * Loads the content of your inventory into a table
  * Allows altering the quantity of (or to entirely turn off) resources that the solver is allowed to consider
  * Can reset back to inventory state at any time
  * Click "Submit" at any time to pass your selection to the Recipes tab
* Recipes
  * Computes which recipes can be made with the selected resources
  * Fetches prices from Universalis for your homeworld for each recipe
  * Allows setting the conversion rate of scrips into gil to allow automated pricing of collectables
  * Allows altering the price of (or to entirely turn off) recipes that the solver will use
  * Can reset back to initial computed prices and selection
  * Click "Solve" at any time to pass your selection to the solver and move to the Results tab
* Results
  * Displays progress of the solver
  * When finished, will display the optimal integer solution found in a table
